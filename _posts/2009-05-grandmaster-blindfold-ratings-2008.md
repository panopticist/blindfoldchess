How would grandmasters rank in blindfold ability if we had official ratings? This blog provides an informal ranking of top players based on their demonstrated blindfold chess skills as of 2008.

The ranking considers both the number of blindfold games played simultaneously and the quality of play. While players like Anand, Kramnik, and Morozevich regularly compete in blindfold tournaments, their abilities differ from the simultaneous display specialists.

This evaluation provides insight into which modern players have the strongest blindfold skills.
