Alexander Alekhine was widely considered the strongest blindfold player of all time. This blog presents three of his brilliant blindfold games that showcase his tactical genius even when playing without sight of the board.

Alekhine's blindfold games often featured the same attacking flair and deep combinations that characterized his regular play. These games demonstrate that top players can maintain their quality even under blindfold conditions.

The games presented here include annotations highlighting Alekhine's remarkable calculating ability.
